## How It Works Technically

Wipely's image super-resolution uses **neural network Super-Resolution algorithms**, with the model running directly in your browser (accelerated by WebAssembly and WebGL).

In simple terms:

1. Upload image to browser locally
2. AI model analyzes image content, identifying edges, textures, and details
3. Generate high-resolution version based on learned image features
4. Export image at 2x resolution, preserving original format (PNG/JPG/WebP)

The entire process doesn't require internet connection, and images are never uploaded to any server.
